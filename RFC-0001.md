1. Vision

Kotoba is NOT a vocabulary app.

Kotoba is NOT a grammar app.

Kotoba is an AI-native Japanese Learning OS.

Its goal is to build a personal Japanese Knowledge Graph and continuously evaluate, update and visualize the learner's mastery.

2. Design Principles

These are immutable.
